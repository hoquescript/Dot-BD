What is KARBAR?

KARBAR is friendly Business Accounting Software to handle all the requirements under financial and accounting system. With KARBAR, you will get the complete solution for Computerization of your business accounting and financial system.

 

What can you do with KARBAR?

There are a lot of features in KARBAR you can work on. Some of its features have been mentioned here.

  • Only Daily Vouchers entries to be required to produce all the reports like Daily Transaction, Individual Ledgers, Bank Statement, Trial Balance, Profit and Loss Statement, Balance sheet etc.
  • Other customizable reports can also be produced as needed.
  • Able to produce Accounts Sector / Cost-Center / Profit Center wise as well as consolidated way
  • Automatic error control such as detecting double entry error or check for reasonableness
  • Five Level Chart of Accounts
  • Reports such as comparatives statements between Budget vs Actual, Last Year vs Current Year are also produced.
  • KARBAR is available on single or multi-user computers.
  • KARBAR can handle accounts for small, medium or large organizations with or without multi sector.
  • Financial Accounting like-Balance sheets, Profits and loss Statements, Cash Flow, Trial Balance etc.
  • Journal Report.
  • Cash / Bank Book.
  • Group Balance Statement.
  • Income/Expenses Analysis Report.
  • Comprehensive Budgeting, Auditing and Security Control.
  • Multi Company and Multi Division Accounts.
  • Import/Export of Data.
  • General Ledger.
  • Sector / Cost-Center / Profit-Center Account.
  • Multilevel Security and much more.
